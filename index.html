Lasagna

Description

Lasagna, also known as lasagne, is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made in very wide, flat sheets. The same-named Italian dish is made of stacked layers of lasagna alternating with fillings such as ragù, béchamel sauce, vegetables, cheeses, and seasonings and spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 lb lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic. crushed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 4 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 lb mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic in a large saucepan until well browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and water. Season with sugar, 2 tbsp parsley, basil, 1 tsp salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours with occasional stirring.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna sheets in boiling water for 8-10 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a large bowl, combine ricotta cheese with eggs, the remaining 2 tbsp parsley and 1/2 tsp salt.
  5. Preheat oven to 190 degrees Celsius.
  6. Assemble the lasagna by spreading 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish. Arrange 6 lasagna sheets lengthwise over the sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the lasagna sheets. Top with 1/3 of the mozarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over the mozzarella and sprinkle with 1/4 cup of Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat the layers and top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil and bake in the oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes.
  8. Remove the lasagna from the oven and let cool for 15 minutes before serving.
